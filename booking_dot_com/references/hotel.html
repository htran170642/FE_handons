Property description

Located in the heart of Brussels, Hotel Metropole is a historic landmark that has been welcoming guests since 1895. Occupying a magnificent Art Nouveau building on Place de Brouckère, this iconic property blends 19th-century grandeur with modern comfort.

The hotel features 241 beautifully appointed rooms and suites, each decorated with period furniture and offering every modern amenity. Guests can enjoy the renowned L'Alban Chambon restaurant, the historic Café Métropole, and the elegant lounge bar.

The Grand' Place is just a 5-minute walk away, and the hotel is conveniently located near Brussels Central Station, making it the ideal base for exploring the Belgian capital.
